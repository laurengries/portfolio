    • For this project, I was able to create custom Instagram Story Templates for the Big Ten Tournament National Champions. This piece was made in Adobe Photoshop to promote official merch for the entire roster of the Uconn Woman's and Florida Men's Basketball teams after the national championship wins. I started with a clean vertical canvas, added in cutout images of the T-shirts and jersey, and arranged them in a bold, balanced layout. I decided on color palettes based on the team colors. To make it pop, I used handwritten-style arrows and circles to guide the eye, and mixed fonts for energy and emphasis. The athlete’s name on the side adds a personal touch, and the championship badge ties the whole thing together. Everything was aligned and polished for social media use and saved as a high-res PNG.
