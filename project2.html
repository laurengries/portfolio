    • This series was developed to promote the launch of officially licensed Tennessee Baseball NIL jerseys through the NIL Store. Each graphic was designed to be flexible for use across email, social media, and in-store displays—maintaining visual consistency while adapting to different aspect ratios and content priorities. The campaign centers on high-resolution mockups of the new jerseys, showcasing both home and away styles in signature Tennessee orange and white. Clear, commanding headlines and bold typography draw immediate attention to the release message: “New Baseball Jerseys Available!” A consistent blue background and varsity-inspired typeface reflect the brand's collegiate identity while ensuring readability and high impact. Smart objects and modular layers were used to streamline asset swaps and maintain efficiency across the entire campaign rollout.
