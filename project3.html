    • This promotional poster series was created to recruit college athletes to participate in Campus Ink’s NIL (Name, Image, Likeness) merchandising program at Iowa State University. The designs highlight officially licensed apparel options available through the NIL Store, emphasizing inclusivity and profitability for all athletes. Two variations were developed for flexible marketing use across print and digital platforms. Both designs feature a bold headline—“Sell Your Merch, Get Paid”—to instantly communicate value, paired with clear calls to action and a scannable QR code for easy access. Apparel mockups were arranged in a grid for visual balance and product emphasis, while the Iowa State brand identity was maintained through use of official colors, fonts, and logos.
